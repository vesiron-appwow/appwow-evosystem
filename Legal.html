Legal & Trust

Platform Positioning

AppWow operates as a directory and discovery service.

It does not host third-party applications, process transactions, or act as a marketplace. Applications featured on AppWow are accessed directly from their original publishers.

Data & Privacy

AppWow is built with a privacy-first philosophy.

  • No unnecessary tracking
  • No sale of personal data
  • Minimal analytics for platform integrity only

Users can browse AppWow without accounts or identification.

Content Responsibility

Each application featured on AppWow remains the responsibility of its publisher.

AppWow does not modify, control, or interfere with third-party application behaviour. Clear removal and governance processes will apply where standards are not met.

Governance & Policy Direction

Formal policies, terms, and governance frameworks will be published as the platform evolves.

These will focus on:

  • Fair access
  • Abuse prevention
  • Transparency
  • User trust

AppWow's priority is long-term credibility, not short-term scale.
