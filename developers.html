Publishing on AppWow

AppWow is designed to support Instant App developers who build focused, web-based applications using modern standards such as PWAs.

The platform favours:

  • Clear purpose
  • Fast load times
  • Privacy-respecting design
  • Real-world utility

This is not a volume marketplace. It is a curated environment.

How AppWow Is Different

Traditional app stores act as gatekeepers.

They impose:

  • Complex submission processes
  • Revenue extraction models
  • Algorithmic visibility controls

AppWow takes a different approach.

It is built as a discovery and visibility layer, not a distribution choke point. Developers retain ownership, control, and independence.

Instant Apps vs Native Apps (High Level)

Instant Apps:

  • Run directly in the browser
  • Work across devices
  • Update instantly
  • Require no installation

Native apps:

  • Require downloads
  • Are tied to platforms
  • Add friction for users

AppWow exists because Instant Apps are now mature enough to stand on their own.

Roadmap (Informational Only)

Future phases of AppWow will introduce:

  • Structured submission workflows
  • Developer analytics
  • Optional monetisation support

These features are not active yet and are intentionally staged to preserve platform quality and trust.
