LINK3

LINK3 ‚Äî Structured Discovery System based upon three distinct stages.

LINK3 controls how content (listed items) is/are discovered and how users (AppWow registered members) are allowed to act on the content listed.

It separates who can see from who can act, allowing AppWow to scale discovery without becoming a marketplace.

Visibility and Action Are Separate

On AppWow, seeing something and acting on it are purposely two different things.

LINK3 defines:

  • who can view content at what stage
  • who can act on it at what stage

Action rights increase only where validation and registration level allow.

Registration Adds Validation

AppWow registration creates a validation layer.

Registered users:

  • are identifiable within platform rules
  • operate under defined behavioural standards
  • can be granted controlled access and action rights

This validation is what makes LINK3 possible.

Stage 1 ‚Äî Controlled Discovery

Who can see:
  • Stage 1 registered persons
  • Stage 2 registered users
Who can act:
  • Stage 1 registered persons only (Professional Buyers / Dealers / Collectors etc whom are registered as Stage 1 members)

Stage 1 is used where serious or sensitive engagement requires tight control and Listers require prompt action with best offers.

Stage 2 ‚Äî Registered Discovery

Who can see:
  • Stage 1 registered persons
  • Stage 2 registered users (General Dealers / Traders / Private Buyers etc)
  • myLynx-Gold members
Who can act:
  • Stage 1 registered persons
  • Stage 2 registered users
myLynx Gold:
  • view and save only

Stage 2 expands visibility while keeping action rights deliberate.

Stage 3 ‚Äî LinxMart Display

Who can see:
  • everyone (registered or not)
Who can act:
  • AppWow registered users / members

LinxMart is a display centre with maximum visibility and controlled engagement.

How LINK3 Works

LINK3 allows AppWow to show content widely without giving everyone the same powers.

All Stages are time controlled, usually:

  • Stage 1 = 3 Days
  • Stage 2 = 3 Days
  • Stage 3 = 28 Days

Visibility increases through the stages.

Action ability is always controlled by registered member status.
