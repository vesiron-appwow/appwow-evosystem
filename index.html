About AppWow

What is AppWow?

AppWow is a global discovery and display platform dedicated to Instant Apps (IA's) ‚Äî modern, installable Progressive Web Apps (PWA's) that run instantly in a browser, without downloads, app stores, or platform lock-in.

AppWow exists to make discovering, trusting, and using high-quality web-based applications simple, safe, and consistent worldwide.

The AppWow Evosystem

AppWow operates as part of the AppWow Evosystem ‚Äî an intentionally evolving framework designed to support:

  • Instant App discovery
  • Developer visibility and credibility
  • Sector-specific discovery portals
  • Cross-device compatibility
  • Long-term global scalability

The Evosystem allows AppWow to grow organically without disrupting existing users or developers.

Built for Global Growth

AppWow is designed to be:

  • Platform-agnostic
  • Internationally deployable
  • Region and language extensible
  • Developer-friendly
  • Privacy-respecting

Instant Apps listed on AppWow may be accessed globally, subject only to developer-defined limitations.

Developers

AppWow for Developers

AppWow is open to AppWow Registered Developers who create Progressive Web Apps (PWA's) suitable for instant access and public discovery.

AppWow is a display and discovery platform, not an app store or marketplace.

Basic Listing Requirements

Submitted Instant Apps must:

  • Be a valid Progressive Web App (PWA)
  • Run reliably in modern browsers
  • Use HTTPS
  • Clearly identify the developer or publisher
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Contain no malicious, deceptive, or unlawful content

AppWow reserves the right to reject or remove listings that fail to meet these standards.

LINK3

A structured, fair-access disposal and acquisition framework within AppWow.

What is LINK3?

LINK3 is a tiered interaction framework available to AppWow registered users, designed to manage the disposal and acquisition of items in a controlled, transparent way.

It is not a marketplace or shop.

It is a process layer that governs who can see what, when, and how.

Who Can Use LINK3?

  • All AppWow registered users may access LINK3 at appropriate levels
  • myLynx-Gold enables expanded participation
  • Higher-value or sensitive actions are gated to protect users and sellers

LINK3 supports both:

  • Disposal (offering items)
  • Acquisition (expressing interest or intent)

Why LINK3 Exists

LINK3 is designed to:

  • Reduce spam, abuse, and bad-faith behaviour
  • Protect private individuals and serious sellers
  • Introduce fairness and order into discovery-led transactions
  • Separate display from engagement

LINK3 Levels

  • Structured access stages
  • Graduated permissions
  • Clear behavioural rules
  • Platform-wide enforcement logic

Note: Detailed LINK3 mechanics and level structures are explained in dedicated documentation. LINK3 functionality is integrated across CarLinx, HomeLinx, and other Evosystem platforms where applicable.
